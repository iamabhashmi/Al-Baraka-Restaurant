Our Story

Three Decades of Authentic Flavor

Al-Baraka Restaurant was founded in 1995 by Haji Muhammad Barkat, a man who believed that food is more than sustenance — it is love, culture, and memory served on a plate.

What started as a small dhaba with five tables has grown into one of Lahore's most beloved restaurants, serving over 500 customers daily across dine-in, takeaway, and delivery.

Every recipe has been passed down through the family, refined over generations, and prepared fresh every morning using ingredients sourced from trusted local farms.

100% Halal — certified and guaranteed
Fresh ingredients sourced daily from local markets
Family recipes unchanged since 1995
Free home delivery within 5km
Private dining available for events & gatherings
